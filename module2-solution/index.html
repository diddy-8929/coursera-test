Sweet Items

Kaju ki Katli

Made with cashew nuts and milk, this barfi is a childhood favourite of many. So easy and yet so divine, no Indian celebration is complete without it. Skip the store-bought variety, and cook this in the comfort of your home.

Gajar ka halwa

It is our ultimate winter indulgence, and this recipe is here to help you make a delicious portion for yourself in the comforts of your kitchen! Made with grated carrots, khoya, milk, sugar, ghee and lots of chunky nuts, this wholesome and rich dessert will leave you craving for more.

Sandesh

One of Bengal's much loved sweetmeat, the cottage cheese delight is oh-so easy to make at home too. Flavoured with cardamom and kesar, this delicious sweet is a hit in every occasion.
